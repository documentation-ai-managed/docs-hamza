## Overview

Start organizing your docs with a project space in Hamza.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboard">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.hamza.com` and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click the "New Project" button on the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details">
    Enter a project name and (optional) description. Then click save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Documenting">
    Your project is ready! Add documentation or invite your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
